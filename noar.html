Noar

Singer / Songwriter

What's up, I'm Noar, from Leyte Philippines. A Indie Filipino Singer, Songwriter, Multi-instrumentalist, Record Producer & Soloist. I've been writing songs since I am 14 years old. and learn guitar and other instruments to collaborate my music. I am passionate with my works and do love performing infront of everyone and singing my written songs. I have created my band a year 2009 and name it "Letter to Eve". and get disbanded in a year 2015. and now present day, I've been busy honing my own band "EVENJULY" which actually.. Yes! A Oneman band. and it's Great!.
